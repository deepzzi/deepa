ARJITH SINGH

FAVORITE SINGER

Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian playback singer and music composer
The recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award and seven Filmfare Awards,
he has recorded songs in several Indian languages.
Singh began his career when he participated in the contemporary reality show Fame Gurukul in 2005.
He received widespread recognition with the release of successful tracks"Tum Hi Ho" and "Chahun Main Ya Naa" from Aashique 2 in 2013.
